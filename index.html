How to Play

Drive forward: On mobile, push the joystick up to accelerate, down to brake/reverse, left/right to steer. On desktop use WASD or Arrow keys. Space boosts.

Race flow: Pick a map, laps, and rivals. In multiplayer, everyone chooses a preferred map, then the wheel spins and chooses only after the host starts the vote.

Wrenches: Collect glowing wrenches on track and win races to earn more. Use them in the shop and garage upgrades.

Fullscreen: Tap the fullscreen button. If Safari blocks it, use the Share menu and “Add to Home Screen” for a near-fullscreen experience.

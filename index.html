How To Play

Drive forward through checkpoints and finish laps. Collect glowing wrenches, hit boost pads, use jumps/tunnels/shortcuts, and avoid off-road slowdown.

Mobile: push joystick up to drive forward, left/right to steer, down to brake/reverse. Tap Boost for turbo.

Desktop: WASD/Arrow keys drive, Space boosts, Escape pauses.

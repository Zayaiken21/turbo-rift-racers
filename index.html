How to Play

Desktop

WASD or Arrow Keys drive. Space boosts. Escape opens pause.

Mobile

Drag the left joystick. Up accelerates, down brakes/reverses, left/right steer. Tap the glowing boost button.

Race Rules

Follow route arrows, pass checkpoints in order, finish all laps, and collect glowing wrenches.

Garage

Spend wrenches on vehicles, paint colors, trails, and upgrades. Progress saves locally on your device.
